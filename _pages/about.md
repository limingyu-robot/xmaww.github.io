I am currently a Master's student in the School of Instrument Science and Engineering at Southeast University, advised by Prof. Aiguo Song. Prior to this, I received my Bachelor's degree from Southeast University in 2024 and spent time as a Visiting Student at the Advanced Robotics Lab at Great Bay University, working with Prof. Michael Yu Wang.

My research interests lie at the intersection of **robotic perception, vision-tactile fusion, and dexterous manipulation**. I am particularly passionate about developing teleoperation systems for robotic dexterous hands and leveraging reinforcement learning algorithms, such as PPO and MAPPO, to solve complex control and sim-to-real transfer problems within Isaac Sim and real-world environments. My ultimate goal is to enable robots to interact with the physical world as dexterously and robustly as humans do.
